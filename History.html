It all began in 1977, when Guru Gary and Guru Gru had a vision: goldfish deserved enlightenment too.

In a haze of incense and jazz flute, the first underwater retreat was born in a kiddie pool in Berkeley.

Since then, our facility has grown from a bathtub to a 12-tank spiritual oasis. Thousands of aquatic minds have been calmed, one ripple at a time.
